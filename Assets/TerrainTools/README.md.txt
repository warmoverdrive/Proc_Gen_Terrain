Add a Custom Terrain script to your terrain objects and begin generating procedural terrains.

Functionality of some algorithms depend on extremely low numbers in the sliders.

Increasing tree and detail density will cause extreme performance hits in editor - unsure of build stability at time of writing.

Packaged with Unity Standard Assets (Water prefabs and Projector shader effects), and Holistic 3D Assets (Cloud, Skydome, Shoreline assets).
